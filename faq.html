Frequently Asked Questions

Simple answers to common questions about PayGain, insurance planning, renewal support, policy benefits, and the customer process.

At PayGain, we understand that financial planning can feel confusing, especially when it involves insurance, premiums, long-term benefits, and renewal support. This FAQ page answers common questions in simple language so you can make a more informed decision before starting your financial planning journey.

Section 1: About PayGain

What is PayGain?
PayGain Multi Services Pvt. Ltd. is a financial services company that helps customers plan for financial stability, family protection, passive income, and long-term wealth creation through insurance and related financial solutions.
What does PayGain help customers with?
PayGain helps customers understand and choose financial solutions for life insurance, health insurance, motor insurance, marine insurance, and other financial products.
Who is PayGain mainly for?
PayGain is suitable for Indian families, salaried people, business owners, parents, and individuals who want to plan for child education, retirement, medical emergencies, regular income, and family security.
What is PayGain’s main goal?
PayGain’s goal is to help common citizens of India move towards a financially stable and comfortable life by offering practical financial planning options.
Is PayGain an insurance company?
PayGain works as a financial service provider and presents insurance-related solutions through trusted insurance partners. The insurance policy is issued by the respective insurer.
Which companies are PayGain’s partners?
PayGain works with insurance partners shown in its brochure, including Bajaj Life, Niva Bupa Health Insurance, and Tata AIG Insurance.

Section 2: Services and Products

What services does PayGain offer?
PayGain offers support and guidance for life insurance, health insurance, motor insurance, marine insurance, and preference share-related opportunities.

PayGain helps customers understand life insurance strategies through partner insurance providers. These plans may include life coverage, yearly payouts, tax benefits, and future wealth creation depending on the chosen policy.
Does PayGain provide health insurance?
Yes, health insurance is one of the services PayGain offers.
Does PayGain provide motor insurance?
Yes, motor insurance is one of the services PayGain offers.
Can PayGain help me plan for my child’s future?
Yes. PayGain’s planning approach can help customers explore options for securing their child’s education and future needs.
Can PayGain help me build a retirement corpus?
Yes. PayGain helps customers explore long-term planning options for retirement income and financial security.

Section 3: Life Insurance and Policy Benefits

What is PayGain’s life insurance strategy?
PayGain’s life insurance strategy is designed to help customers create passive income, maintain life coverage, and plan for future wealth through insurance-based financial planning.
What are the main benefits of the policy shown in the brochure?
The brochure lists benefits such as tax benefit, life coverage, premium waiver benefit, death benefit during the policy term, guaranteed yearly payout, and future wealth creation.
What does premium waiver benefit mean?
Premium waiver benefit generally means that in certain situations, future premiums may be waived as per the policy terms. The exact conditions depend on the selected policy and insurer rules.
What is death benefit?
Death benefit means the amount payable to the nominee if the insured person passes away during the policy term, subject to the terms and conditions of the policy.
What is guaranteed yearly payout?
Guaranteed yearly payout means a fixed yearly amount that may be paid as per the policy illustration and policy terms.
Are the returns tax-free?
Some plan illustrations mention tax-free benefits. However, tax treatment depends on current tax laws, policy type, customer profile, and applicable government rules. Customers should consult a tax advisor or PayGain representative.
Are the benefits guaranteed?
Some benefits may be guaranteed as per the selected policy illustration. Customers should check the official policy document and benefit illustration before purchasing.

Section 4: Renewal Support

What is PayGain Renewal Support?
PayGain Renewal Support is a support structure where PayGain may help customers with selected renewal premiums based on the selected plan, eligibility, and applicable terms.
How does the 12 premium and 6 premium support system work?
In one plan example, the customer has a 12-year premium-paying term. PayGain supports 6 premiums, which may reduce the customer’s effective burden.
Does this mean I only pay 6 out of 12 premiums?
The brochure states that by choosing the maximum benefit, the customer needs to pay only 6 premiums out of 12 premiums. However, customers should confirm eligibility, support terms, and conditions directly with PayGain.
How much renewal support can PayGain provide?
In the example shown, PayGain Renewal Support can go up to ₹12,00,000.
Is PayGain Renewal Support guaranteed?
Renewal support is presented as part of PayGain’s plan structure. Customers should confirm the exact terms, eligibility, documentation, and conditions directly with PayGain before making a decision.
Why does PayGain offer Renewal Support?
PayGain offers Renewal Support to help customers continue their policy and reduce the pressure of future premium payments, subject to applicable terms and eligibility.

Section 5: Cost, Premiums, and Returns

How much do I need to invest?
PayGain presents different plan slabs. Examples include annual premium options like ₹1 lakh, ₹2 lakh, ₹3 lakh, ₹4 lakh, and ₹5 lakh, depending on the customer’s financial capacity and goals.
In the ₹2 lakh premium example, how much does the customer pay?
The example shows a customer paying ₹2,00,000 annually for 12 years, making the total premium ₹24,00,000.
What is the actual cost after support in the example?
In one example, the brochure shows total premium of ₹24,00,000, moneyback from Bajaj of ₹11,00,000, PayGain Renewal Support of ₹12,00,000, and actual cost of ₹1,00,000. This is based on the illustration and should be verified with official documents.
What does “You Gain ₹57L+” mean?
This refers to the illustrated benefit shown in the brochure. Customers should check the official benefit illustration and policy documents before purchasing.
Can I choose a plan based on my budget?
Yes. PayGain presents different premium slabs so customers can explore options based on their budget, income, and financial goals.
Can I stop the plan midway?
This depends on the selected insurance policy. Stopping midway may affect benefits, returns, and support. Customers should check surrender terms and policy conditions before buying.
What happens if I miss a premium?
This depends on the policy rules. Some policies may have a grace period, while others may lapse if premiums are not paid. Customers should check the insurer’s policy terms.

Section 6: Customer Safety and Process

Is my money safe with PayGain?
PayGain positions itself as a trusted and ethical financial partner. However, customers should always review official policy documents, insurer details, payment process, receipts, and terms before making payments.
Who issues the insurance policy?
Insurance policies are issued by the respective insurance companies. PayGain assists with planning, service, and support.
Should I pay directly to PayGain or the insurance company?
Customers should confirm the correct payment process directly with PayGain. For insurance products, payments should be made only through official and traceable channels, with proper receipts.
Will I get official policy documents?
Yes. For any insurance policy, customers should receive official policy documents from the insurer. Customers should read and save these documents carefully.
What should I check before buying?
Customers should check the premium amount, premium-paying term, policy term, life cover, guaranteed and non-guaranteed benefits, renewal support terms, surrender value, maturity benefit, death benefit, tax rules, exclusions, and conditions.
How do I start with PayGain?
Customers can contact PayGain through phone or email, discuss their financial goals, understand suitable options, and review the plan details before making a decision.
What documents are usually needed?
Common documents may include PAN card, Aadhaar card, photograph or selfie, signature, income details, occupation details, nominee details, mobile number, and email ID.
